Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

1. Why did you choose these specific cities?
I selected these cities—Paris, Tokyo, New York, and Istanbul—because they each offer something unique and exciting. From culture and history to modern life and beauty, they represent my biggest travel dreams.

2. Have you visited any of these places yet?
No, I haven’t visited them yet. This website is a way to express my hopes and goals for future travel. I plan to visit them one day when the opportunity comes.

3. What inspired you to create this website?
This website is part of an HTML assignment, but I wanted to make it more personal and interesting by sharing something I'm passionate about—travel.

4. Are the pictures and descriptions real?
The descriptions are written by me, based on what I’ve learned and read. The images (if any) are used from free sources to show what the places look like.

5. Will you add more cities in the future?
Yes, I would love to expand this website in the future and include more destinations as I learn more about web development.
